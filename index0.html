About the Lab

The Neuroimaging & Biophysics Lab (NBL) is dedicated to advancing our understanding of brain structure and connectivity through innovative computational approaches. Our research focuses on developing cutting-edge methods for diffusion MRI analysis and white matter tractography.

We combine expertise in neuroimaging, biophysical modeling, and computational neuroscience to create tools that enable researchers and clinicians to better understand the human brain.

Research Areas

Diffusion MRI

Advanced diffusion imaging methods for mapping brain microstructure and tissue properties.

Tractography

Novel algorithms for reconstructing and analyzing white matter fiber pathways in the brain.

Biophysical Modeling

Computational models linking MRI signals to underlying tissue biophysics and microstructure.
